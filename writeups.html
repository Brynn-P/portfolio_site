My Write-ups

Being able to craft well worded and clear write-ups is a skill within its own right. Although many people dont fully understand why this is important (I myself am still learning). There are many benifits to a well formed write up. This includes being able to look back into my own process and learn about myself. Learn about my own strenghts and weeknesses, learn where to improve(there is always room to improve) Beyond that it also is a valuable resorce that i can use to look back at when I am stuck and find solutions to similar problems
