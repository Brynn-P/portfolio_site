Choose your suffering

life is full of choices, some beleive life is suffering so we must do so with purpose.

Choose your suffering.

I know this seems rather counter intuitive because when we thinking about it, any kind of suffering is not pleasant, but as much as we may want it all to be so called smooth sailing, suffering is a necessary part of life.

This I believe is something we all already know deep down even if we have not spent a lot of time thinking about it.

So what do I mean when I bring this up?

Well there are many things in life that are unavoidable such as death, suffering and taxes 😋

But it’s not to say that it’s all doom and gloom.

If something like suffering is unavoidable you may be wondering how on earth do we turn that into a positive, well I think that we do that by choosing our suffering, suffering with purpose if you will.

An example I can use is in my own life, in my profession I spend many weeks far from my friends and loved ones, and sometimes I have infuriatingly long days where everything seams to go the exact opposite of the way I had wanted it to, but even on the worst days, I am still in a place that I once could only dream of being in, to get here it took sacrifices and trials that even though i have passed through them at times still hurt, at times I still suffer them.

And this process shall be repeated again as I work towards my next goals, I will struggle and I will in some degree suffer, but I will do so with purpose.

So if to suffer in life is an inevitable thing, rather choose to suffer towards a greater goal, for if you have none, you will still suffer but to no end.

“HOW DOES IT HELP…TO MAKE TROUBLES HEAVIER BY BEMOANING THEM?” - SENECA
