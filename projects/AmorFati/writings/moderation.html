Moderation

We, like nature need balance, found in moderation.

Moderation

I am sure everyone reading this has heard the saying moderation is key, but is it always?

Well I suppose In many ways it is, but In some it is not.

Moderation for me is a form of self discipline such as putting down that bar of chocolate because you have already had half😋 or choosing to have a glass of water instead of another whiskey, but for things such as these it is easier to draw the line or at-least know you are taking it too far, but I also feel it can get more complicated than that.

Moderation is key, but what about love, how do you love in moderation, is it still love when you do so?

I think it is important to note that I am not talking about setting boundaries as that for me is an entirely different art, but I believe that some things should be done wholly and fully without holding back, a bit of an all or nothing approach

Now I am also not saying that there is a right or a wrong approach to all of this and I have much to learn and my opinion too may change in the future, but this is how I see it at the moment.

For me you can’t love but only a little bit, with conditions and safety lines attached, loving in moderation because that for me is not love, rather some form of attachment.

You can’t love in moderation because moderation is logical and very little about love actually makes sense.

You can’t love in moderation because love does not come with pre-requisites and conditions, it either is or it is not I don’t believe there is a middle ground

When I speak about love in this piece I think it is important to acknowledge the many forms of love, from self love, to the love of family and friends, and romantic love.

I also think it is just important to also know that just because one has love in one of these many forms does not mean you cannot set healthy boundaries for oneself and love at the same time.
