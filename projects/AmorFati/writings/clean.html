Clean vessle

We all want good things in life that we can maintain and grow, but do we prepare for it?.


If the vessel is not clean, whatever you pour in goes sour.

I heard this whilst listening to the books of epistle by the Roman poet Horace.

This saying has played in my mind on repeat since i heard it and I wanted to break down what it means to me.

We all go through many things in this crazy and wonderful experience called life.

With all the things we go through and deal with, many of these things leave lasting marks on us and they are not all good, but alas this is part of being alive.

The things we pour into ourselves, the joy we seek, the love we give to others and hope to receive ourselves, the time and effort we put into ourselves and our passions are often soured by holding onto things in the past.

We are our own vessels and until we clean ourselves (forgive and heal ourselves) the very things we actively seek out are spoilt by the things we want to leave in the past.

Leaving these things in the past is not enough, we have to address them and work through them and clean out ourselves if we want to be able to enjoy the future that is waiting for all of us to seize.

We are the only ones who can make this life of ours beautiful for ourselves, we are also the only ones who can break it down.

Take back the power over your life, heal yourself, learn to love yourself and let the joys that are meant to be yours flow towards yourself.
