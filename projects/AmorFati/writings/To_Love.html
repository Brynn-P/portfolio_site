To love is sometimes to loose.

We have all loved in one way or another, it is a human thing to do, but we too have all lost.

To love is sometimes to loose.

We have all been through some kind of heart break, be it a partner, a friend, a family member, or sometimes all three and more.

We also have all seen or heard the saying "if you truly love something you must set it free, if it comes back it was ment to be."

But few explore what happens when it does not, and it is not ""ment to be" if you will.

One thing I believe is that once you love, you can walk away and hide your emotion but it does not mean it is not there.

Sometimes love burns and other times it just smolders on, but it is never gone.

This for me has been one of the hardest lessons to learn, learning to accept and live with all the emotion that you want to share and give but can't.

All the would be and should be moments that never will be the way you might have envisioned it, as the never ending continuance of time and life moves on past all the milestones that you once strived for.

It's ok to feel hurt, it's ok to miss and even crave the missed moments but it's not ok to let it take over and stop you from finding new moments for yourself.

Make no mistake, healing hurts, allot.

But much like a physical wound you must open it up and clean it out, every day, until all you are left with is a scar, and then you must learn to live with that scar.

(last bit was taken from a documentary I watched recently call "That which I love destroys me")

The point is, it's not something that just goes away if you try and bury it, rather quite the opposite.

Sometimes you don't even see how much it is affecting you until more damage has been done.

costing you your peace of mind, relationships or professional life

As much as healing may hurt, it is worth it, and you are worth healing!

While you may need a helping hand along the way or even just a shoulder to cry on....but to get to that help, you have to start.

Starting moving forward, towards a place of help and that can only be done by you.

So if you know there is healing that you still need to start cleaning out, let this be you call to action.

The sooner you start the process the sooner there is more space for light in your life.
